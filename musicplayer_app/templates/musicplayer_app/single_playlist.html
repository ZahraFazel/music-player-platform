miraculous music platform

The Miraculous is a platform for sharing and streaming a broad range of certified music. The Miraculous was prepared by the order of "Automated Software Engineering Laboratory" of the Sharif University of Technology. You can listen to your favorite music, and by activating your premium account, you can download them.

about us

The Miraculous is the final project of the "System Analysis and Design" course, presented by Prof. Abbas Heidarnouri. The developers of this system are Atena Saghi, Zahra Fazel, and Mahdi Ghaznavi. The supervisor of this project is Ali Mousavi.
